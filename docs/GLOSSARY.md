# Glossary

## Incident
An unplanned interruption or degradation of service.

## Investigation
The process of determining contributing factors and probable causes.

## Evidence Package
A structured collection of telemetry and contextual information used for AI-assisted reasoning.

## RCA
Root Cause Analysis.

## SLI
Service Level Indicator.

## SLO
Service Level Objective.

## ADR
Architecture Decision Record.

## RFC
Request For Comments.

## MTTD
Mean Time To Detect.

## MTTA
Mean Time To Acknowledge.

## MTTI
Mean Time To Investigate.

## MTTR
Mean Time To Resolve.
